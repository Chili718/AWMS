Our Clients

We serve our clients in several different capacities from one time ad hoc projects to long term assignments. Client projects include product development, pricing (i.e. manual rating, credibility rating, and experience rating), policy language, actuarial rate filings, reserve valuation, data analytics, experience reporting & forecasting (i.e. utilization, price, cost, and loss ratio), financial projections, profit forecasting, and business modeling. We serve client companies of all sizes in over 35 states across the country.

Our clients include insurance companies, third party administrators (TPAs), consulting firms, marketing organizations, reinsurance companies, private equity firms, informatics companies, preferred provider organizations (PPO's), claim review companies, and health care providers.
